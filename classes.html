Discover Your Ideal Pilates Practice

At Stretch Society, we're dedicated to helping you move with intention and strength. Whether you're looking to build a stronger core, improve flexibility, or find balance, our classes are designed to support you at every step of your Pilates journey. With a mix of classic mat-based techniques, dynamic reformer sessions, and balance-focused strength classes, we offer something for everyone. Explore our class options below and find the perfect fit for your body and goals.

Mat Pilates

Experience the fundamentals of Pilates in our classic mat class, designed to build core strength, enhance flexibility, and improve posture. Perfect for all levels, this class offers a mindful, full-body workout that challenges stability and control.

Reformer Pilates

Discover the transformative power of the reformer! This class combines resistance and movement for a dynamic workout that tones muscles, increases flexibility, and enhances body alignment. Ideal for those wanting a deeper, muscle-targeted Pilates experience.

Core & Balance

Challenge your core, improve stability, and build full-body strength. Our Strength & Balance class focuses on controlled movements and balance exercises to help you feel strong and centered both on and off the mat.

One-On-One Sessions

Get personalized attention with a private Pilates session tailored to your goals, skill level, and body's needs. Perfect for focused progression and mastering form

Duo Sessions

Enjoy a customized, semi-private session with a friend or partner! Duo sessions allow for a shared, personalized Pilates experience, guided by our expert instructors.

Special Workshops

Dive deeper into specific Pilates techniques and wellness topics. Our workshops offer a focused environment to enhance skills, knowledge, and confidence in your practice.

Community Events

Connect with others in our Pilates community through events that inspire wellness, mindfulness, and movement. Join us for seasonal gatherings, themed classes, and more.
